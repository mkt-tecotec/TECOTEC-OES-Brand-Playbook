# TECOTEC OES — Brand Playbook Design Context

## Users
- **Primary:** Marketing & Communications team, application engineers writing content
- **Secondary:** Sales team (proposals, email), external partners (agency, co-marketing)
- **Context:** B2B/B2G optoelectronics — procurement officers, R&D managers, lab heads

## Brand Personality
- Expert, Trustworthy, Dedicated, Current, Solution-oriented
- NOT: lecturing, jargon-heavy, passive, hollow, trend-chasing

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Light mode with navy/orange accents
- **Mood:** Authoritative, precise, technically credible — NOT flashy or consumer-oriented
- **Typography:** Manrope (display), Source Sans 3 (body), JetBrains Mono (specs)
- **Colors:** Navy `#114466` (authority), Orange `#FF9900` (accent), Blue `#146EB4` (links)
- **Pattern:** Dot scatter motif inspired by photon/particle concept from OES logo

## Anti-references
- Consumer tech landing pages (too flashy)
- Generic SaaS marketing sites (too bubbly)
- Cyber/hacker dark themes (AI slop territory)
- Stock photography heavy corporate sites

## Content Constraints
- Technical specs must come from official datasheets
- No superlatives without evidence
- Vietnamese primary, English for technical terms
- All external content requires engineering review before publish
